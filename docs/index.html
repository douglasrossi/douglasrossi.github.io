Douglas Rossi Melo

Professor @ Univali · Researcher @ LEDS · Engineer @ Webphy

Bachelor in Computer Engineering from the University of Vale do Itajaí (2008), Master in Applied Computer Science from the University of Vale do Itajaí (2012), and PhD in Electrical Engineering from the Federal University of Santa Catarina (2020). He is currently a Professor at the University of Vale do Itajaí, a Researcher at the Laboratory of Embedded and Distributed Systems, and a System-on-Chip Architect and FPGA Engineer at Webphy Systems. His topics of interest are Systems-on-Chip, Networks-on-Chip, Hardware Accelerators, and Fault Tolerance.
